York U Hospitality
Brand & Mobile App

Project Overview

York University needed a comprehensive hospitality brand system that would unify their campus dining experience while creating a modern, student-friendly mobile ordering platform. The challenge was to design a system that felt contemporary and accessible while maintaining institutional credibility.

The YU Eats brand was developed to create a vibrant, energetic identity that resonates with students. The bold red color palette and dynamic pattern system became the foundation for both environmental graphics and digital touchpoints across campus.

Mobile Experience

The mobile app design prioritizes ease of use and speed. Students can browse menus, customize orders, and schedule pickups between classes. The interface uses clear typography, intuitive navigation, and the signature YU pattern system to create a cohesive brand experience.

Key features include mobile ordering, real-time order tracking, customizable dietary preferences, and seamless payment integration. The design reduces friction in the ordering process, allowing students to spend less time waiting and more time engaging with campus life.

Brand System

The YU Eats brand system extends beyond digital. Environmental graphics, signage, and promotional materials all utilize the distinctive pattern language and bold color palette. This creates recognition and consistency across every student touchpoint.

The modular pattern system allows for flexibility while maintaining brand integrity, making it easy to adapt materials for different dining locations and seasonal campaigns.
