Sarnia Arena
Environmental Design

Project Overview

The Sarnia Arena required a complete environmental redesign to transform outdated spaces into premium hospitality and recreation destinations. The goal was to create distinct identities for each area while maintaining a cohesive visual language throughout the facility.

The redesign focused on three key areas: luxury suites for premium guests, a social dining hub, and a youth recreation space. Each zone needed to appeal to its specific audience while elevating the overall arena experience.

Youth Hive

The Youth Hive transforms a previously underutilized space into a vibrant recreation zone designed specifically for younger visitors. Bold geometric patterns and high-energy graphics create an engaging environment that encourages social interaction and active play.

Dynamic hexagonal shapes and a blue-and-gold color palette establish a modern identity that resonates with youth culture. The space includes recreational amenities and flexible areas for gatherings, making it a destination within the arena.

Premium Hospitality

The luxury suites received a complete transformation with sophisticated materials, custom lighting, and refined environmental graphics. Rich wood tones, textured walls, and warm ambient lighting create an upscale atmosphere for corporate clients and premium ticket holders.

Branded elements are integrated subtly throughout the space, maintaining elegance while reinforcing the arena identity. The design balances comfort and sophistication, offering a premium experience that matches modern hospitality standards.
